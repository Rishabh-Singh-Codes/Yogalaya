About Us

Spiritual growth can be a messy process! We offer a tried and true formula from ancient India that will help you navigate the currents. There are four important components to the process.

  • 25% Teacher Effort
  • 25% Student Effort
  • 25% Community Effort
  • 25% Time and Patience
When all these ingredients come together the student is well supported in their efforts.

We also offer a retreat centre where you can relax and enjoy services:-
  • Ayurveda medicines
  • Massage
  • Sports activities
  • Fitness Tours
  • Excursions
  • Nutrition planning
  • Cooking classes
